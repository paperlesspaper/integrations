# Your Life in Weeks

Shows a week-by-week lifetime grid inspired by the TRMNL "Your Life in Weeks" recipe.

## Settings

- `birthDate`: Birth date in `YYYY-MM-DD` format.
- `lifeExpectancy`: Number of years to draw in the grid.
- `title`: Heading shown on the display.
- `showAge`: Toggles the age and progress statistic cards.
- `showLegend`: Toggles the legend below the grid.

The integration is static and does not call an external API.
